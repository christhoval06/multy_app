## ENGLISH

### 1. Who we are
Multy is an educational mobile app designed to help children practice multiplication tables.

Developer contact:
- Email: `christhoval06@gmail.com`
- Website: `https://s.christhoval.dev`

### 2. Data we collect
By default, Multy is designed to work with local data stored on your device.

The app may store:
- Quiz attempts and results
- Learning progress and basic statistics
- Onboarding and app preferences

We do not require account creation to use the app.

### 3. How data is used
Stored data is used to:
- Show your learning progress
- Generate quiz results and statistics
- Improve your in-app experience

### 4. Data sharing
We do not sell your personal data.

The app does not share local progress data with third parties by default.  
If you choose to open external links (e.g., website, email, social profiles), those services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

### 5. Children’s privacy
Multy is intended for educational use by children, under parent/guardian supervision when applicable.

We do not intentionally collect unnecessary personal information from children.  
If you believe personal data was shared unintentionally, contact us and we will review and address the request.

### 6. Data retention and deletion
Your progress data remains on your device unless you delete the app data or use the in-app delete option.

The app includes an option to delete all local data from Settings.

### 7. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect data stored by the app.  
However, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

### 8. Third-party services
Multy may use platform/system services needed for app functionality (for example, opening links or emails).  
Any third-party service you access has its own terms and privacy policy.

### 9. Your rights
Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data (access, correction, deletion, etc.).  
You can contact us to request support about your data.

### 10. Changes to this policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above.
